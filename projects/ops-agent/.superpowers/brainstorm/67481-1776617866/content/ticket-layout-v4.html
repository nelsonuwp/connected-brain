Fix suggestion
Based on APTUM-36100 and APTUM-35800, this is consistent with a BGP route advertisement failure after the maintenance window. The VPN concentrator lost its return path, causing IKEv2 negotiation to fail silently.

Check order: (1) BGP neighbor state on the core router — run show bgp summary and look for a downed peer. (2) Confirm the static route to the VPN concentrator IP is present. (3) Re-advertise the default route from core if missing. Resolution in APTUM-36100 was a one-line BGP config fix — took ~15 min under an emergency change window.
Description
Since the scheduled maintenance window on the night of Apr 13–14, approximately 40 employees at Northbridge Financial are unable to connect to the office VPN gateway. The error reported is "IKEv2 negotiation failed." The issue appears to affect all VPN users simultaneously and began immediately after the maintenance window closed.
