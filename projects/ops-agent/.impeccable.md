# ops-agent Design Context

## Target Audience
Internal Aptum support staff — primarily Adam and ops team — reviewing JSM tickets, investigating issues, and generating LLM-drafted responses.

## Use Cases
- Look up a specific ticket by key
- Browse and filter the ticket queue
- See customer history and hardware context in the sidebar
- Generate LLM drafts for fix suggestions and customer replies

## Brand
Aptum brand guidelines apply:
- Primary: Lavender #7D7AE8, Navy #081820, Off-white #F8F7FA
- Font: Geist (already loaded from aptumlogos.pages.dev)
- Tone: Practical, clear, professional — "plain and simple, people first"

## Aesthetic Direction
Refined utilitarian — this is a dense internal ops tool, not a marketing page.
Data-dense but breathable. Clean visual hierarchy. No decoration for its own sake.
Left-aligned, structured, information-forward.
