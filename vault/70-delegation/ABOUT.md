# 70-delegation  ·  Delegation

Delegation briefs. One file per significant delegation.

**Contains:**
- Outcome statement (what done looks like — not a task list)
- Why it matters (1 paragraph business context)
- Authority boundary (can decide vs. must escalate)
- Delegation level (L1–L5)
- Check-in cadence and escalation triggers

**Delegation levels:**
- L1: Tell me what to do, I decide
- L2: I decide, keep me closely informed
- L3: I decide, you approve before execution
- L4: You decide within constraints, escalate only outside them
- L5: You own the outcome, I support

**Rules:**
- Required when: timeline >1 week, or outcome isn't self-evident
- Tactical handoffs (<1 week, obvious outcome) don't need a formal brief
- Write it for the recipient — it's their document, not your tracker
- When complete: mark done, do not delete (it's a record)
