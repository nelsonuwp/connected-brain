# 20-context/business  ·  Reference → Business

Business model and org context for LLM injection.

**Contains:**
- OSOM model definition (federated service structure, P&L logic)
- Org structure (who owns what, reporting lines)
- Strategic context (current priorities, constraints, why major decisions were made)
- Glossary (internal terms, what "service manager" means here)

**Rules:**
- Required context for: initiative specs, delegation briefs, cross-service planning
- Prevents LLMs from giving generic advice disconnected from how this business operates
- Update when org structure or strategic direction meaningfully shifts
