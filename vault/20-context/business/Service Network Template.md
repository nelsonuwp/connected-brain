## Lifecycle

| Discovery | Alpha | Beta | Live | Decommissioned |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

**Lifecycle Definitions:**
* **Discovery:** Understanding the problem that needs to be solved
* **Alpha:** Testing potential implementations
* **Beta:** Building and preparing for live usage
* **Live:** Actively used and meeting operational goals
* **Decommissioned:** Retired or being retired

## Evolution

| Novelties | Bespoke, or custom | Product | Commodity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

**Evolution Definitions:**
- **Novelties:** New or unique services
- **Bespoke:** Custom-built for organizational needs
- **Product:** Standardized internal or external productized service
- **Commodity:** Widely available, interchangeable service
