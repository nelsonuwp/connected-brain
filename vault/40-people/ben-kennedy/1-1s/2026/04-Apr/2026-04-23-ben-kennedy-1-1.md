**B. Team utilization under "How We Are Measured"**

The current metrics table covers uptime (99.999%), margin (20% soft target), service delivery timelines (TBD), and compliance. What's missing: **team utilization** — how Ben's engineers' time is being spent.

Network Engineering operates in two modes: reactive (escalations, incidents, vendor tickets, customer troubleshooting) and proactive (infrastructure build, automation, PoCs, compliance work). The split between these matters because:
- Too much reactive = the team is fighting fires and can't invest in infrastructure maturity
- Too little reactive = demand is light, or escalations aren't reaching them (which could mean other teams are absorbing the work)

Proposed metric to add: **Engineer time allocation — reactive vs. proactive (% of hours per period)**. Even an informal tracking mechanism is better than nothing. What would the target split look like? 30% reactive / 70% proactive? Ask Ben what feels right for his team's current state.
