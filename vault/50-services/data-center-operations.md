# Data Center Operations

## Purpose
The Data Center service provides foundational services for physical operations: data center real estate management, facility management, critical infrastructure management, physical security, rack and stack, remote hands capability, cabling, and physical inventory management. It supports both internal and external customers and aids in the design, implementation, and operations of physical office and data center installations.

## Responsibilities (Declared)

- Right-size customers' physical operating environment so that expenditure is kept to a minimum.
- Identify and resolve issues quickly so that service quality is maintained.
- Manage infrastructure operations for customers so that they can focus on their business.
- Meet security and compliance standards customers specify so that they can protect the integrity of their business.
- Provide remote hands assistance for provisioning, de-provisioning, and troubleshooting of physical infrastructure so that customers can ensure their business is updated and working properly.
- Ensure real estate portfolio is covered by appropriate leases so that resources are appropriately allocated.
- Apply industry standards and best practices regarding redundancies to solutions so that customers may understand and evaluate risk.
- Maintain visibility into what assets exist and where they are so that customers can make better sourcing decisions.
