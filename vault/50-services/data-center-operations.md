## Service Description

The Data Center Ops service provides physical facility and infrastructure management for internal and customer colocation environments, enabling customers and internal teams to operate without managing the complexities of physical data center operations. It delivers rack and stack, remote hands, cabling, physical security, critical infrastructure management, and asset inventory control so that customers can maintain a right-sized, compliant, and continuously available physical operating environment.

## User Needs

- As a **Customer**, I need my physical operating environment right-sized so that my expenditure on space and power is kept to a minimum.

- As a **Customer**, I need issues in my physical environment identified and resolved quickly so that service quality is maintained and business impact is minimized.

- As a **Customer**, I need my physical infrastructure operations managed on my behalf so that I can focus on my business.

- As a **Customer**, I need my security and compliance standards applied to my physical environment so that I can protect the integrity of my business.

- As a **Customer**, I need remote hands support for provisioning, de-provisioning, and troubleshooting of physical infrastructure so that my environment stays current and operational without requiring my team on-site.

- As a **Customer**, I need confidence that my real estate is covered by appropriate leases with adequate runway so that I can plan resource allocation without risk of displacement.

- As a **Customer**, I need industry-standard redundancy practices applied to my physical infrastructure so that I can evaluate and understand risk to my business.

- As a **Customer**, I need accurate visibility into my physical asset inventory so that I can make better sourcing and capacity decisions.
