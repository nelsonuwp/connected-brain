## Service Description

The Service Desk provides L2/L3 and NOC support for managed hosting, colocation, and networking environments, enabling customers to operate their infrastructure with confidence that incidents are handled and resolved without requiring their own intervention. It delivers SLA-governed ticket management, infrastructure-layer incident ownership, hyperscaler triage, proactive monitoring, and business continuity support so that customers experience consistent service quality across day 2 operations, and cloud platform issues are routed efficiently to the right team.

## User Needs

- As a **Customer**, I need my service to operate reliably and securely so that I can focus on growing my business without disruption.

- As a **Customer**, I need all tickets, whether submitted by me or generated by monitoring, to be acknowledged and responded to within defined SLA or SLO timeframes so that I have confidence my issues are being prioritised and addressed correctly.

- As a **Customer**, I need my infrastructure supported and monitored so that I can focus on my business rather than the underlying environment.

- As a **Customer**, I need confidence that my business continuity strategy can be executed successfully so that I can protect my business integrity.

- As a **Customer**, I need visibility into the health of my physical and logical infrastructure so that I can plan my team's work accordingly.

- As a **Customer**, I need to meet industry compliance standards so that I can achieve required certification and protect the integrity of my business.

- As a **Customer**, I need transparent and timely communication about changes and incidents so that I can plan for expected changes and respond to unplanned events effectively.

- As a **Management Team member**, I need visibility into how resources are spending their time so that I can measure the profitability of the service.

- As a **Service Desk team member**, I need proactive communication about service execution, customer value opportunities, and any information required to maintain customer satisfaction so that I can deliver a consistent and high-quality service.

- As a **Service Desk team member**, I need to be notified of product and service updates and recommendations for expanding the service catalog so that I can keep customers informed of relevant capabilities.

- As a **Service Desk team member**, I need access to certifications and training so that partner tier status and specialization requirements are maintained.

- As a **Service Desk team member**, I need defined handoff processes from project delivery teams so that transitions from implementation to managed operations are seamless and customers experience no gap in support.
