# Service Desk

## Purpose
The Customer Care service comprises support, engineering, architecture, and delivery capabilities to develop, deliver, and support infrastructure solutions that meet the diverse needs of customers' performance, security, and regulatory requirements.

## Responsibilities (Declared)

- Help customers define infrastructure solutions so that they can focus on the business value supported by the solution.
- Operate the service reliably and securely, meeting performance, continuity, and compliance requirements so that customers can focus on growing their business without disruption.
- Acknowledge and respond to all service desk tickets (customer-submitted or automatically generated through monitoring alerts) within the defined SLA or SLO response times based on assigned priority level.
- Provide real-time ticket status and history through a unique customer portal; define clear escalation paths and procedures for each priority level; provide automated notifications for ticket status changes and updates.
- Support customers' infrastructure so that they can focus on their business rather than the underlying infrastructure.
- Ensure customers' business continuity strategy can be executed successfully so that they can protect their business's integrity.
- Operate without disruption so that customers' businesses continue to operate.
- Enable customers to understand the health of their physical and logical infrastructure so they can plan their team's work accordingly.
- Meet industry compliance standards so that customers can achieve required certification and protect the integrity of their business.
- Ensure vendors/partners support transparent and timely communication so that customers can plan for expected changes and respond to unplanned incidents effectively.
- Support the Management Team in understanding where resources are spending their time so that they can measure the profitability of the LoB.
- Provide proactive communication about important information about the execution of the service to the Customer Care Team.
- Be notified of product and service updates and recommendations for expanding and enhancing the catalog; achieve certifications and training to ensure partner tier status and specialization requirements are met.
- Collaborate with defined processes for seamless transitions from project delivery to managed services.
