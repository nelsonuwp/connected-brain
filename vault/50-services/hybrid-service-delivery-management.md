# Hybrid Service Delivery Management

## Purpose
The Hybrid Service Delivery Management (HSDM) team provides end-to-end delivery and relationship management, enabling customers to focus on their core business and strategic outcomes. It delivers project execution, deliverable ownership, stakeholder alignment, proactive communication, and ongoing customer engagement to ensure alignment between service delivery and evolving customer business needs. Customers can raise requests via the Support Portal (24x7) or by contacting HSDM directly through designated communication channels.

## Responsibilities (Declared)

- Provide a single point of contact for all delivery-related questions and clear visibility into project progress so that external customers can stay informed without coordinating with multiple teams and focus on their core business operations.
- Provide clear project hand-off documentation and delivery timelines so that Professional Services can transition customers smoothly and focus on new engagements without worrying about ongoing support issues.
- Provide early visibility into upcoming service transitions and customer-specific requirements so that Managed & Support Services can prepare resources and maintain service continuity without disrupting other customer commitments.
- Provide project execution support and delivery coordination so that Hybrid Solution Architecture team members can focus on technical leadership and customer solution design rather than project management overhead and stakeholder communications.
- Provide regular status updates on project health and customer satisfaction to Commercial Account Managers so that they can manage client expectations, identify expansion opportunities, and address concerns before they escalate.
- Provide accurate project cost tracking and resource utilization data to Finance so that they can forecast budgets, track profitability, and support pricing decisions for similar engagements.
- Orchestrate the end-to-end delivery process from project initiation through closure, including planning, stakeholder management, risk mitigation, and resource allocation; serve as the linchpin between Cloud Platform Solution Architecture, technical teams, and clients (Synthesized from: SDET-HSDM + CLP-CPSD)
- Manage and communicate deliverables, risks, project timeline, ROI, and issues; manage and oversee complex projects and planning of team and customer meetings (Synthesized from: SDET-HSDM + CLP-CPSD)
