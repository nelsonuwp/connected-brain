## Service Description

Hybrid Service Delivery Management (HSDM) provides end-to-end delivery and relationship management, enabling external customers and internal delivery and commercial teams to execute and track cloud platform projects and focus on their core work. It delivers project execution, deliverable ownership, stakeholder alignment, proactive communication, ongoing customer engagement, and orchestration from project initiation through closure so that projects are delivered on time, within budget, and to quality standards and customers and teams stay informed and aligned. Customers can raise requests via the Support Portal (24×7) or by contacting HSDM directly through designated communication channels.

---

## User Needs

**As an External Customer,** I need a single point of contact for all delivery-related questions and clear visibility into project progress so that I can stay informed without coordinating with multiple teams and focus on my core business operations.

**As a Professional Services team member,** I need clear project hand-off documentation and delivery timelines so that I can transition customers smoothly and focus on new engagements without worrying about ongoing support issues.

**As a Managed & Support Services team member,** I need early visibility into upcoming service transitions and customer-specific requirements so that I can prepare resources and maintain service continuity without disrupting other customer commitments.

**As a Hybrid Solution Architecture team member,** I need project execution support and delivery coordination so that I can focus on technical leadership and customer solution design rather than project management overhead and stakeholder communications.

**As a Commercial Account Manager,** I need regular status updates on project health and customer satisfaction so that I can manage client expectations, identify expansion opportunities, and address concerns before they escalate.

**As a Finance team member,** I need accurate project cost tracking and resource utilization data so that I can forecast budgets, track profitability, and support pricing decisions for similar engagements.

**As a Solution Engineer,** I need scope of services and participation in internal kickoff to discuss technical requirements and add input to the sales-to-delivery checklist so that the SOW aligns with delivery capacity and the engagement can be executed.

**As an Account Manager,** I need a warm handover from sales to the Service Delivery Manager and a two-week timeline for internal communication before kickoff so that the delivery team can deliver the project on time and on budget.

**As an External Customer,** I need meet-and-greet, scheduled kickoffs, and weekly status reports via call or email so that I have a trusted point of contact and can escalate when needed.
