## Service Description

The Hybrid Service Delivery Management service provides end-to-end delivery and relationship management, enabling customers and internal teams to focus on core business and strategic outcomes. It delivers project execution, deliverable ownership, stakeholder alignment, proactive communication, and ongoing customer engagement so that service delivery remains consistently aligned with evolving customer business needs.

## User Needs

- As a **External Customer**, I need **a single point of contact for all delivery-related questions and clear visibility into project progress** so that **I can stay informed without coordinating with multiple teams and can focus on my core business operations**.

- As a **Solution Engineer**, I need **to participate in internal kickoffs and provide technical scoping and requirements** so that **delivery teams can align on the statement of work and plan engagements accurately from the outset**.

- As a **Commercial Account Manager**, I need **regular status updates on project health and customer satisfaction** so that **I can manage client expectations, identify expansion opportunities, and address concerns before they escalate**.

- As a **Professional Services team member**, I need **clear project hand-off documentation and delivery timelines** so that **I can transition customers smoothly and focus on new engagements without carrying ongoing delivery responsibilities**.

- As a **Managed & Support Services team member**, I need **early visibility into upcoming service transitions and customer-specific requirements** so that **I can prepare resources and maintain service continuity without disrupting other customer commitments**.

- As a **Hybrid Solution Architecture team member**, I need **project execution support and delivery coordination** so that **I can focus on technical leadership and solution design rather than project management overhead and stakeholder communications**.

- As a **Finance team member**, I need **accurate project cost tracking and resource utilization data** so that **I can forecast budgets, track profitability, and support pricing decisions for similar engagements**.
