# 50-services  ·  Business → Internal Services

OSOM service delivery units. One note per managed service you operate internally.

**These are HOW you deliver. Not WHAT you sell.**

**Contains:**
- Service definition (what it delivers, to whom, target margin)
- Current state — updated every Friday (revenue, margin, SLA compliance, open JSM issues)
- Cross-service dependencies
- Active initiatives (links to 30-initiatives)
- Risks for next 90 days
- Service manager development (current delegation level, building toward)

**Rules:**
- Friday update is mandatory — this becomes your operating review artifact
- Do NOT confuse with 51-catalog — services are how you operate, catalog is what you sell
