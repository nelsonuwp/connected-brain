# 10-thinking  ·  Thinking

Active thinking space. Ideas that survived the inbox but aren't initiatives yet.

**Contains:**
- Problem framing notes
- Options analysis
- Half-formed ideas being developed

**Rules:**
- Think mode: use external LLMs (Sonnet/Opus) here to pressure-test and structure
- Never send directly to Cursor — mixed abstraction levels confuse execution LLMs
- Promote to 30-initiatives when the idea has: an owner, an outcome, and a timeline
- Promote to 20-context when the idea produces reusable knowledge
- If a note hasn't moved in 2 weeks: promote it or delete it
