# Advisory (Assess)

The advisory motion is the entry point into every Aptum customer relationship. Seven structured assessments diagnose the customer's environment, quantify risk, and produce a roadmap. Each assessment is a fixed-fee, t-shirt sized engagement (Small through Extra Large) led by a Solution Architect. Assessments run between 20 and 300 hours depending on scope.

Every assessment produces the evidence that justifies investment in the Execute and Operate motions that follow. The assessment converts a generic pitch into a specific, evidence-based conversation: not "you should buy managed patching" but "here are the 14 unpatched servers running end-of-life operating systems that showed up in your assessment."

Every dollar spent in assessment revenue generates two to three dollars in follow-on revenue within 12 months. The highest-return assessments (Cloud Repatriation, Operational Maturity) generate 10 to 20x follow-on multipliers.

## Assessment-to-Service Pathway Map

### 1. Infrastructure Risk & Readiness Assessment

**What the assessment typically finds**: Aging hardware (EOL CPUs, SSDs, firewalls), unsupported operating systems, deferred maintenance, capacity gaps, single points of failure.

**Natural follow-on services**:

| Priority | Service Layer | Why | Typical Deal Size |
|----------|--------------|-----|------------------|
| Primary | **Consulting** | Migration planning from legacy to modern infrastructure | $25K-$150K (project) |
| Primary | **Dedicated Hosting / Infrastructure** | Hardware refresh -- lease Dell R660s, SAN upgrades, replace EOL gear | $3K-$15K/month (recurring) |
| Secondary | **Managed Cloud (OS & Above)** | Ongoing patching, monitoring, backup for the refreshed environment | $2K-$10K/month (recurring) |
| Secondary | **Facility Services** | If customer needs to move from aging colo to modern facility | $2K-$8K/month (recurring) |

**Conversion talk track**: "The assessment showed you have X devices past end-of-life and Y unsupported operating systems. Here's a phased refresh plan that eliminates those risks. We can lease modern hardware, manage the migration, and take over ongoing operations."

## The seven assessments

| Assessment | Customer trigger | Assessment leads to |
|---|---|---|
| Infrastructure Risk and Readiness | EOL hardware, deferred maintenance, aging servers | Hardware refresh (Execute) then Reactive managed services |
| Hybrid Cloud | Workload placement uncertainty, hybrid sprawl, VMware pressure | Architecture consulting (Execute) then Reactive or Proactive |
| Security Posture and Compliance | EOL firewalls, CVE exposure, audit findings, compliance gaps | Security remediation (Execute) then Proactive with MDR |
| Cloud Repatriation | Cloud overspend, flat usage with rising bills | Repatriation project (Execute) then Proactive on Aptum IaaS |
| Operational Maturity | IT team at 80%+ reactive capacity | Managed services transition (Execute) then full Proactive |
| App and Platform Modernization | Legacy infrastructure under modern apps, immature CI/CD | Platform build (Execute) then Proactive managed platform |
| Well-Architected Review | Unreviewed cloud estate, cost overruns, security concerns | Architecture remediation (Execute) then Reactive or Proactive |
