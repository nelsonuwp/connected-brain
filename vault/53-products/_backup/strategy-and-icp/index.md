# Strategy and ICP

This section contains the two documents that underpin every product, pricing, and go-to-market decision at Aptum. Read these before navigating the rest of the space.

The Aptum Product Strategy defines the company's position as a hybrid cloud managed services provider, the three-motion operating model (Assess, Execute, Operate), the two revenue pillars (managed services and Aptum IaaS), and the roadmap. It explains what Aptum is optimizing for and what it has explicitly stopped doing.

The Aptum ICP defines the single unified ideal customer profile: a mid-market organization between 50 and 2,000 employees with hybrid infrastructure complexity that exceeds their internal IT team's capacity to manage. It includes firmographic anchors, behavioral signals, the full customer journey story, what a relationship is worth at each stage, and the geographic focus. Every assessment entry point, product fit decision, and upsell motion should be evaluated against this profile.

Both documents are versioned and dated. Always confirm you are reading the current version before drawing on them for commercial conversations or product decisions.
