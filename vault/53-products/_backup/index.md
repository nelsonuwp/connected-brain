# Product Strategy — PRD Space

This space is the authoritative source for Aptum's product strategy, infrastructure catalog, managed services, and professional services documentation. It is maintained by the Product team and used daily by commercial, engineering, and delivery teams.

The space is organized into five sections. Strategy and ICP defines the strategic model and the customer Aptum is built to serve. Infrastructure Products covers everything Aptum delivers at the infrastructure layer — colocation, servers, connectivity, IaaS, and public cloud. Aptum Portal covers the control plane that customers use to consume those products. Managed Services covers the engagement tiers and addon catalog that sit on top of the infrastructure. Professional Services covers the advisory (assessment) and execute (implementation) motions.

Every engagement Aptum runs should follow the Assess to Build to Operate model: start with an assessment that produces evidence, move through an execution project that acts on the findings, and land in a recurring managed services relationship.

Content in this space syncs automatically to and from the connected-brain vault via the bridge script. Files or folders prefixed with an underscore are excluded from sync and are safe to use as internal working documents.
