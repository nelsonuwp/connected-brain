# Addons

Aptum managed service addons are purchasable services that stack on top of any infrastructure commodity. Each addon is independently sellable and priced per endpoint or asset per month. How Aptum operates each addon depends on the customer's engagement tier: at Reactive, Aptum runs the tooling and the customer stays in the decision loop; at Proactive, Aptum owns the outcome.

## Compatibility

Not every addon is available on every infrastructure product or at every engagement tier. The key constraints are: the Dedicated tier has no addon access; BCP Planning and Compliance Reporting are Proactive tier only; Managed Backup is Proactive-only on Colocation. Infrastructure monitoring, security, backup, and OS management addons are broadly compatible with all infrastructure products except Colocation (where Aptum does not own the hardware). Full compatibility detail is on each category page.

## The nine addon categories

The addons are organized into nine functional groups. Each group has its own page defining the specific named Aptum products within it and a service guide covering operational delivery across all products in that group.

Monitoring and Observability covers the three monitoring tiers: Essentials (Zabbix), Advanced (LogicMonitor), and Application (Datadog). OS and Platform Management covers OS Patching (Automox) and Application Platform Patching. Data Protection and Recovery covers Managed Backup (Veeam), DRaaS, and BCP Planning. Security Services covers seven products from firewall policy through MDR and compliance reporting. Cloud and Hybrid Connectivity covers Hybrid Interconnects and FinOps. Logging covers Operational Logging. Network and Delivery covers Managed DNS (Cloudflare) and Load Balancing (L7). Application Services covers Database Tuning and DevOps Monitoring. Managed Productivity covers Managed M365.
