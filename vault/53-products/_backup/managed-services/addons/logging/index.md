# Logging

## Aptum Operational Logging

Centralized collection, retention (three-month default), and customer access for infrastructure and platform operational logs. Tooling options: Datadog Logs or Elastic Cloud. At Reactive tier, log collection agents are deployed, ingestion is automated, retention is managed, and the customer has log access and query capability. At Proactive tier, Managed Cloud configures alert rules on log patterns, manages compliance retention, and conducts log analysis for incident investigation.

This product covers infrastructure and platform logs only. Custom application log pipelines — where a customer needs their application's own log output centralized and analyzed — require a separate PS engagement. That is not in scope for Operational Logging.

Owners: Managed Cloud (log management and alert configuration); Compute Platforms (infrastructure log standards). Cost: Datadog Logs approximately $0.15 CAD per GB ingested plus approximately $3.75 CAD per million log events. Elastic Cloud approximately $0.14 CAD per GB per month for storage.
