# Execute (Implement)

The Execute motion acts on assessment findings. These are project-based engagements: migrations, hardware refreshes, platform builds, security remediations, architecture redesigns, managed services transitions, and DR design and implementation. Every Execute engagement should trace back to an assessment finding and have a clear line of sight to an Operate outcome.

Execute engagements are scoped by a Hybrid Solutions Architect (HSA) and delivered by HSDM coordinating cross-functional teams from across the Aptum service network. Revenue range: $5,000 to $300,000 per project.

## The three commercial structures

**Fixed SOW** defines scope, milestones, and payment schedule upfront. This is the standard model for bounded Execute projects where the environment is known (typically from the preceding assessment), the work can be specified, and delivery milestones can be defined. The assessment that precedes the project has already mapped the environment and identified the risks, making scoping faster and delivery more predictable.

**Time and Materials (T&M)** is hourly billing used for smaller tasks, change requests, and work that cannot be fully scoped upfront. T&M is appropriate when the scope is genuinely uncertain or when the customer needs a small amount of expert time without a formal project structure.

**Evolve** is a monthly recurring squad retainer. A cross-functional squad of Cloud Architects, Cloud Specialists, and Cloud Operators works in two-week Scrum sprints on mutually agreed business objectives, operating alongside a Solution Architect (Product Owner) and Project Manager (Scrum Master). The commercial structure is a Minimum Monthly Fee (MMF) — billed at the greater of the MMF or actual hours consumed that month. The engagement auto-renews unless either party provides 30 days written notice. Evolve is the right structure for multi-month platform engineering, DevOps, and advisory work where the backlog evolves sprint by sprint and a fixed scope cannot be defined in advance.

## Types of work Aptum delivers under Execute

Cloud migrations (P2V, V2V, on-premises to Aptum IaaS or hyperscaler), cloud repatriation projects (workloads from hyperscaler to Aptum Dedicated Cloud or Private Cloud), hardware refreshes (EOL server replacement: spec, procure, build, migrate, decommission), security remediations (firewall replacement, OS upgrades, hardening, compliance alignment), platform builds (Kubernetes implementation, CI/CD pipeline, container platform), architecture redesigns (well-architected remediation, cost optimization, governance implementation), managed services transitions (operational handoff from customer IT team to Aptum ops teams), and DR design and implementation (failover architecture, runbook development, first DR test).
