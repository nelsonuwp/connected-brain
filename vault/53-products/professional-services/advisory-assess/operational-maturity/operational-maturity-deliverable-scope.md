# Operational Maturity Assessment -- Deliverable Scope

> **SOW-ready language.** Copy the relevant size section into the Statement of Work.

---

## Assessment Description

The Operational Maturity Assessment evaluates the customer's current IT operations model, quantifying the cost and effectiveness of their self-managed approach versus a managed services model. The assessment analyzes operational processes, incident patterns, staffing allocation, and tooling to deliver a data-driven recommendation for optimizing how infrastructure is managed.

---

## Objective

To quantify the customer's current operational overhead, identify inefficiencies and risks in their self-managed model, and deliver a comparative analysis showing the cost, quality, and risk trade-offs of their current approach versus a managed services transition.

---

## T-Shirt Sizing

### Small ($5K) -- Quick OpEx Analysis

**Scope**: 1-5 services, small IT team (1-3 people). Focused on cost comparison.

**Deliverables**:
- Current operational model overview (who does what)
- FTE cost allocation analysis (time on infrastructure ops vs. core business)
- TCO comparison: current self-managed costs vs. managed service model
- Recommendation memo: manage vs. outsource (5-8 pages)

**Duration**: 1 week

**Aptum team**: Solution Architect (solo)

**Customer time commitment**: 2-3 hours (interviews + data review)

---

### Medium ($10K) -- Full Operational Review

**Scope**: 5-15 services, full operational review across key domains.

**Deliverables**:
- Operational model review across all key domains (monitoring, patching, backup, incident response, change management)
- Incident pattern analysis (ticket volume, categories, MTTR, repeat incidents)
- Staffing model analysis (FTE cost, skills gap, single-person dependencies)
- Tooling assessment (what tools are in place, what's missing)
- TCO comparison with detailed cost breakdown
- Transition roadmap to managed services (phased)
- Assessment report (15-25 pages)

**Duration**: 2 weeks

**Aptum team**: Solution Architect + 1 specialist (Infrastructure or Operations)

**Customer time commitment**: 5-8 hours

---

### Large ($20K) -- Enterprise Operational Assessment

**Scope**: 15+ services, full process review across all operational domains, organizational analysis.

**Deliverables**:
- Everything in Medium, plus:
- Full operational maturity scoring across all domains (1-5 scale)
- Process documentation assessment (runbooks, procedures, escalation paths)
- SLA performance analysis
- Benchmarking against industry standards
- Organizational recommendations (team structure, roles, skills)
- Detailed transition plan with risk mitigation
- Executive presentation
- Assessment report (25-40 pages)

**Duration**: 3-4 weeks

**Aptum team**: Solution Architect + 2 specialists

**Customer time commitment**: 10-15 hours

---

### XL ($30K+) -- Enterprise Transformation

**Scope**: Enterprise-scale operational transformation. Full redesign of operations model. Custom SOW.

**Deliverables**:
- Everything in Large, plus:
- Future-state operational model design
- Runbook creation for critical processes
- Managed services transition plan with detailed cutover approach
- Change management recommendations
- Executive workshop for alignment on operational strategy
- Board-ready presentation
- Assessment report (40+ pages)

**Duration**: 4-6 weeks

**Aptum team**: Solution Architect + 2-3 specialists + executive sponsor

**Customer time commitment**: 15-25 hours

---

## Out of Scope

- Implementation of operational changes (follow-on engagement)
- Managed services delivery (separate contract)
- Hiring or HR decisions
- Application-level operations assessment
- Security-specific assessments (see Security Posture Assessment)
- Infrastructure hardware assessment (see Infrastructure Risk Assessment)

---

## Customer Responsibilities

1. **Access**: Provide access to monitoring tools, ticketing systems, and operational documentation
2. **Data**: Share ticket/incident data for the last 6-12 months, staffing information, and operational budgets
3. **Availability**: Make IT team members available for interviews (individually, not just management)
4. **Transparency**: Be willing to share how things actually work (not just how they're supposed to work)
5. **Point of contact**: Designate a management-level sponsor and IT team POC

---

## Assumptions

- Customer will provide access to ticketing/incident data within 3 business days of kickoff
- Individual IT team member interviews are essential (not just management perspective)
- Assessment evaluates operational model effectiveness; it does not audit individual performance
- Managed services cost comparisons use Aptum standard pricing at the time of assessment
- Transition roadmap assumes a collaborative transition, not an abrupt cutover

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. All deliverables for the selected size provided
2. Findings presented to customer management and IT team
3. Final report delivered in PDF format
4. TCO comparison delivered in editable spreadsheet format
5. Q&A conducted
