# Security Posture & Compliance Assessment -- Deliverable Scope

> **SOW-ready language.** Copy the relevant size section into the Statement of Work.

---

## Assessment Description

The Security Posture & Compliance Assessment evaluates the customer's infrastructure for security vulnerabilities, end-of-life exposure, firewall policy gaps, and compliance alignment against relevant regulatory frameworks. The assessment produces a prioritized remediation roadmap that enables the customer to address critical risks and meet compliance obligations.

---

## Objective

To identify and quantify security risks across the customer's infrastructure, assess compliance posture against applicable regulatory frameworks, and deliver a prioritized remediation plan with cost and effort estimates.

---

## T-Shirt Sizing

### Small ($5K) -- Basic Security Scan

**Scope**: 1-5 devices, no specific compliance mandate. Basic vulnerability and EOL assessment.

**Deliverables**:
- EOL hardware and software inventory with CVE exposure mapping
- Automated vulnerability scan results with severity ratings
- Top-10 prioritized risk findings with remediation recommendations
- Summary report (5-10 pages)

**Duration**: 1 week

**Aptum team**: Solution Architect (solo)

**Customer time commitment**: 1-2 hours

---

### Medium ($10K) -- Standard Security & Compliance

**Scope**: 5-15 devices, compliance gap analysis against ONE framework (e.g., HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI-DSS).

**Deliverables**:
- Full vulnerability assessment across all in-scope devices
- EOL/EOS inventory with CVE mapping and severity scoring
- Firewall rule review and policy audit
- Compliance gap analysis against one selected framework
- OS vulnerability report (supported vs. unsupported vs. approaching EOL)
- Full prioritized remediation roadmap with cost/effort estimates
- Assessment report (15-25 pages)

**Duration**: 2 weeks

**Aptum team**: Solution Architect + 1 specialist (Security or Network Architect)

**Customer time commitment**: 4-6 hours

---

### Large ($20K) -- Enterprise Security Assessment

**Scope**: 15+ devices, multi-framework compliance, penetration testing, full remediation roadmap.

**Deliverables**:
- Everything in Medium, plus:
- Compliance gap analysis against multiple frameworks
- Manual penetration testing (external and/or internal, scope to be defined)
- Attack surface analysis for public-facing systems
- Remediation roadmap with phased execution plan, timeline, and resource requirements
- Executive risk briefing (presentation)
- Assessment report (25-40 pages)

**Duration**: 3-4 weeks

**Aptum team**: Solution Architect + 2 specialists (Security Architect + Network Architect)

**Customer time commitment**: 8-12 hours

---

### XL ($30K+) -- Enterprise / Bespoke

**Scope**: Enterprise-scale, multiple compliance frameworks, comprehensive pen testing, board-level reporting. Custom SOW required.

**Deliverables**:
- Everything in Large, plus:
- Full internal and external penetration testing
- Security governance and policy review
- Incident response readiness assessment
- Board-ready executive risk report with business impact analysis
- Stakeholder workshop for remediation prioritization
- Assessment report (40+ pages, customized format)

**Duration**: 4-6 weeks

**Aptum team**: Solution Architect + 2-3 specialists + Aptum executive sponsor

**Customer time commitment**: 15-25 hours

---

## Out of Scope

- Remediation implementation (follow-on engagement)
- Ongoing security monitoring or managed detection and response
- Application security testing (SAST/DAST) or code review
- Social engineering testing (phishing simulations)
- Procurement of replacement hardware or security tools
- Legal or regulatory advisory (assessment identifies technical gaps; legal interpretation is customer's responsibility)

---

## Customer Responsibilities

1. **Access**: Provide access to all in-scope infrastructure, firewalls, and management interfaces
2. **Authorization**: Provide written authorization for vulnerability scanning and penetration testing (L/XL)
3. **Compliance context**: Identify which compliance frameworks apply and share any prior audit reports
4. **Availability**: Make security, IT, and compliance stakeholders available for interviews
5. **Point of contact**: Designate a security or IT POC for the duration of the assessment
6. **Notification**: Notify internal teams of authorized scanning/testing activities

---

## Assumptions

- Customer will provide written authorization for scanning within 3 business days of kickoff
- Penetration testing (L/XL) scope will be agreed in writing before testing begins
- Vulnerability scan results reflect the state of the environment at the time of scanning
- Compliance gap analysis identifies technical gaps; it does not constitute legal advice or certification
- Any critical findings discovered during the assessment will be reported immediately to the customer

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. All deliverables for the selected size have been provided
2. Findings presentation has been delivered to designated stakeholders
3. Final report delivered in PDF format
4. Q&A session conducted following findings presentation
5. For L/XL: Penetration testing report delivered separately with technical details
