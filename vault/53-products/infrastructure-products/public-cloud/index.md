# Public Cloud

Aptum delivers Azure, AWS, and GCP to customers as a Cloud Solution Provider (CSP) and managed services partner. The portal provides a single control plane across Aptum IaaS and hyperscaler workloads. Managed services stack on top of public cloud exactly as they do on Aptum-owned infrastructure.

## What Aptum delivers

CSP subscription management (consolidated billing through Aptum, single invoice), cost and usage visibility via Aptum Portal, communication of hyperscaler outages and relevant status to customers proactively, and the full managed services addon catalog available on top of hyperscaler workloads.

## What's always included (Fundamental guarantee)

CSP subscription active, billing consolidated through Aptum, Aptum communicates hyperscaler outages and relevant service status to customers proactively. The hyperscaler's own SLAs apply to the underlying infrastructure. Aptum does not backstop hyperscaler availability guarantees.

## What Aptum manages vs. what the hyperscaler owns

The hyperscaler owns the physical infrastructure, the platform availability guarantee, and the managed services built into the cloud platform. Aptum owns the CSP relationship, the consolidated billing, the portal visibility layer, and all managed operations above the subscription level (OS patching, backup, monitoring, security, FinOps) that the customer purchases as addons.

## Pricing model

Passthrough of hyperscaler costs plus Aptum margin on the CSP relationship. Managed services addons are priced independently on top of the subscription cost. No egress markup — the hyperscaler's own egress pricing applies.

## Integration status

Azure is live in production. AWS is built and available to configure. GCP is built and available to configure. See individual product pages for current status details.

## Managed services available on top

All engagement tiers available. OS Patching, Managed Backup, Advanced Monitoring, Security addons, FinOps, and Hybrid Interconnects are all compatible with public cloud workloads. The Proactive tier with a named Managed Cloud resource is the recommended posture for customers who want Aptum to fully manage their hyperscaler environment.
