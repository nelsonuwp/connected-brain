Below are the notes from Sarah:
### **SUMMARY**

**The session will:**

- Reverse-engineer Aptum's exit, and valuation objectives based on identified strategic and financial buyers
    
- Pressure-test pre-built GTM, technical, and service operating models
    
- Align leadership on specific changes required to close valuation gaps
    
- Conclude with a clear, actionable execution plan and supporting materials for board and investor discussions
    

**Attendees:**

- Executive team (Board + Ian and Sarah)
    
- GTM leadership (Sales, Marketing, Channel) (Marc P. MA??, Fred??, Emma?, Vicki?)
    
- Technical and Services leadership (Adam)
    

**The session is successful if, by the end of Day 3, leadership is aligned on:**

- The exit path and buyer profile Aptum is optimizing for
    
- The operating changes required to support that exit
    
- A credible, data-backed execution plan tied directly to valuation outcomes
    

---

### **PRE-WORK**

**Executive / Strategy**

- Strategic buyer landscape and exit path analysis
    
- Competitive positioning vs likely acquirers
    
- Summary of prior exit process learnings and gaps
    

**Finance (DBRG + APTUM)**

- Current TEV baseline and valuation bridge (current → target)
    
- EBITDA, margin, and growth drivers by segment (from market)
    
- Initial investment scenarios and funding assumptions if anything other than current situation
    

**Sales / GTM**

- Funnel metrics (pipeline, win rates, sales cycle)
    
- NRR, churn, expansion performance
    

---

### **AGENDA**

#### **DAY 1: EXIT, MARKET & VALUE DRIVERS**

**Focus Areas**

- DBRG Valuation Target and timeline summary
    
- Exit paths: strategic buyers vs financial sponsors
    
- Market and competitive landscape relevant to likely acquirers
    
- Buyer-specific valuation drivers (vs generic roll-up logic)
    
- Current performance vs buyer expectations (growth, margin, NRR, scalability)
    
- GTM performance deep dive (NRR, churn, pipeline, CAC)
    
- Revenue engine review: Sales, Marketing, Channel & Evangelism as one system
    

**Day 1 Deliverables**

- Named strategic buyer short-list and exit paths
    
- Buyer-specific valuation thesis
    
- Current GTM scorecard and revenue leakage analysis
    
- Agreed list of value-creating vs non-value-creating activities
    

#### **DAY 2: OPERATING MODEL REVIEW & STRATEGIC ADJUSTMENTS**

**Focus Areas**

- Review of GTM operating model and required adjustments
    
- Review of technical resource alignment and utilization
    
- Dual-purpose technical model: KTLO + growth enablement
    
- Service operating model deep dive:
    
    - Service structure, commitments, and accountability
        
    - Service contracts, SLOs, and margin visibility
        
    - Where resistance exists and what must change
        
- End-to-end customer journey alignment (meet → onboard → run → grow → renew)
    

**Day 2 Deliverables**

- Confirmed changes to GTM, technical, and service operating models
    
- Agreed operating principles and accountability expectations
    
- Clear list of required changes and follow-up workstreams
    

#### **DAY 3 — EXECUTION PLAN & INVESTOR NARRATIVE**

**Focus Areas**

- Gap analysis: current state vs buyer required state
    
- 90 / 180 / 365-day execution roadmap à or up to October 2026 is this is the target exit date
    
- Investment priorities and capital implications (incl. TEV impact)
    
- Operating cadence, governance, and performance scorecard
    
- Synthesis into a coherent exit and execution narrative
    

**Final Outputs**

- Integrated GTM, technical, and service execution plan
    
- Explicit relation between operating changes and valuation impact
    
- Metrics, governance model, and accountability framework
    
- Clear content outline for board and investor materials
