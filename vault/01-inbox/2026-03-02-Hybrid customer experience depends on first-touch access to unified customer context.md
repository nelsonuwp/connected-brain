# Hybrid customer experience depends on first-touch access to unified customer context

## The Idea
Effective support in hybrid environments requires immediate access to a unified customer view at first touch, making this a core operational intelligence capability rather than just a tooling enhancement.

## Why Now
Multiple other ideas you’ve outlined depend on this working. Without it, neither pod models, ownership models, nor HSDM ownership will function effectively.

## Context
Customer context has to be accessible at first touch. If a generalist picks up a ticket from a hybrid customer and has to go hunting for what products they have, what their environment looks like, and who their account manager is — that's where hybrid customer experience falls apart. This is actually an Operational Intelligence problem as much as an org problem. Jorge's team building a unified customer view is directly load-bearing for this.
