# 52-customers  ·  Business → Customers

Customer accounts, segments, and key relationships.

**Contains:**
- Key account profiles (who they are, what they consume, contract tier)
- Segment definitions and characteristics
- Key contacts and escalation history for strategic accounts
- Customer-specific context relevant to service delivery or JSM issues

**Rules:**
- Reference when a JSM issue involves a key account
- This is context, not CRM — detailed account data stays in your CRM
- Link from 50-services when a customer is a major consumer of that service
- Link from 51-catalog when a customer is on a specific product tier
